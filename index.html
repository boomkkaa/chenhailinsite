About Me

Greetings, my name is Chen Hailin and I am a student from Colégio Diocesano de São José (5ª) in the Secondary English Section. I am a skilled athlete hailing from Macau, China, with a specialization in rope skipping. For the past three years, I have served as a coach in this field and have developed a wealth of experience in athletics and coaching. In addition to my coaching work, I also serve as a judge for both the International Jump Rope Union and Macau. My athletic skills have been honed through participation in major competitions such as the "10th Asian Rope Skipping Championships","IJRU 2021 Virtual World Championship Series","The 14th National Student Sports Games", and "Ajru virtual asian championship 2022", as well as numerous local competitions. Outside of athletics, I have worked in student unions for three years and served as a monitor for several years. These experiences have instilled within me a strong sense of responsibility and mission, and have further fueled my passion for sports development.
